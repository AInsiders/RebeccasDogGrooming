How do I book an appointment?

+

You can book an appointment by calling us at 01234 567890, emailing us at hello@anabelgrooming.co.uk, or using our contact form. We recommend booking in advance as our schedule fills up quickly, especially during busy periods.

When booking, please let us know:

  • Your dog's breed and size
  • Preferred service (Bath & Brush, Full Groom, or Puppy Groom)
  • Any special requirements or concerns
  • Your preferred date and time

How long does a grooming session take?

+

The duration depends on the service and your dog's size and coat condition:

  • Bath & Brush: 1.5 - 2 hours
  • Full Groom: 2.5 - 3.5 hours
  • Puppy Groom: 1 - 1.5 hours

Dogs with heavily matted coats or those requiring extra care may need additional time. We never rush the process and always prioritize your pet's comfort and safety.

Do you groom nervous or anxious dogs?

+

Absolutely! I have extensive experience working with nervous, anxious, and reactive dogs. I use gentle handling techniques and take extra time to help your pet feel comfortable.

For anxious dogs, I:

  • Allow extra time for them to settle and get familiar with the environment
  • Use positive reinforcement and treats throughout the process
  • Take breaks as needed to prevent overwhelming your pet
  • Work at your dog's pace and comfort level
  • Discuss any specific concerns or triggers beforehand

Please let me know about your dog's temperament when booking so I can prepare accordingly.

What vaccination requirements do you have?

+

For the health and safety of all pets in our care, all dogs must be up-to-date with their vaccinations:

  • Core vaccines: Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza, and Kennel Cough
  • Puppies: Must be at least 12 weeks old and have completed their second round of vaccinations
  • Annual boosters: Must be current (within the last 12 months)

Please bring your vaccination record or be prepared to provide your vet's contact information for verification. This policy protects all the pets in our care and ensures a safe environment for everyone.

What if my dog's coat is severely matted?

+

Severely matted coats require special care and may incur additional charges of ¬£10-¬£20, depending on the extent of matting. Here's what you should know:

  • Assessment: I'll evaluate the matting and discuss options with you before proceeding
  • Safety first: If matting is too severe, shaving may be the safest and most humane option
  • Skin care: Matted fur can hide skin irritation, which I'll check for and advise on
  • Prevention: I'll provide advice on preventing future matting

Regular grooming every 6-8 weeks helps prevent severe matting and keeps your dog comfortable.

How often should I have my dog groomed?

+

The frequency depends on your dog's breed, coat type, and lifestyle:

  • Long-haired breeds: Every 4-6 weeks for full grooming
  • Medium-haired breeds: Every 6-8 weeks
  • Short-haired breeds: Every 8-12 weeks or as needed
  • Puppies: Every 6-8 weeks to maintain the routine

Regular nail trimming should be done every 3-4 weeks, regardless of coat type. I can help you create a grooming schedule that works best for your dog's specific needs.

What is your cancellation and no-show policy?

+

To ensure fair scheduling for all clients, please note our policy:

  • Cancellations: Please provide at least 24 hours notice to avoid charges
  • Late cancellations: Less than 24 hours notice may incur a 50% service charge
  • No-shows: Will be charged the full service amount
  • Emergencies: Genuine emergencies will be handled case-by-case
  • Rescheduling: We're happy to reschedule with adequate notice

This policy helps us maintain our schedule and ensures availability for all our clients.

What products do you use for bathing and grooming?

+

I use only high-quality, professional-grade products that are gentle on your pet's skin and coat:

  • Shampoos: pH-balanced, hypoallergenic formulas suitable for all skin types
  • Conditioners: Moisturizing treatments to keep coats soft and healthy
  • Specialized products: Available for sensitive skin, allergies, or specific coat types
  • Flea treatments: Medicated shampoos available as add-on services

If your dog has specific allergies or skin conditions, please let me know in advance so I can select appropriate products.

How should I prepare my dog for their first visit?

+

To help ensure a positive first experience:

  • Exercise: A good walk before the appointment can help tire out energetic dogs
  • Feeding: Feed your dog at least 2 hours before the appointment (not on an empty stomach, but not right before)
  • Bathroom break: Make sure your dog has relieved themselves before arrival
  • Information: Bring vaccination records and any special instructions
  • Comfort items: A favorite toy or blanket can help anxious dogs feel more secure

Don't worry about giving your dog a bath beforehand - that's what we're here for!

Do you offer nail trimming as a standalone service?

+

Yes! While nail trimming is included in all our grooming services, I also offer it as a quick standalone service for ¬£10.

Nail trimming appointments typically take 15-20 minutes and include:

  • Careful trimming of all nails
  • Filing to smooth rough edges
  • Quick paw pad inspection
  • Optional nail painting with pet-safe polish (+¬£6)

Regular nail maintenance every 3-4 weeks helps prevent overgrowth, splitting, and discomfort for your dog.

What happens if you notice a health issue during grooming?

+

As part of my service, I perform a visual health check and will alert you to any concerns I notice:

  • Skin issues: Rashes, hot spots, unusual lumps, or irritation
  • Ear problems: Signs of infection, excessive wax, or odor
  • Eye concerns: Discharge, cloudiness, or irritation
  • Dental issues: Severe tartar buildup, broken teeth, or gum problems
  • Mobility issues: Limping, stiffness, or sensitivity

I'll discuss any findings with you and recommend consulting with your veterinarian when appropriate. Early detection can prevent minor issues from becoming serious health problems.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

For your convenience, I accept multiple payment methods:

  • Cash: Always welcome
  • Card payments: Visa, Mastercard, and contactless payments
  • Bank transfer: For advance bookings
  • Mobile payments: Apple Pay and Google Pay

Payment is due at the time of service completion. For regular clients, I'm happy to discuss monthly payment arrangements for multiple pets.
