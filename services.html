Puppy Groom

A gentle introduction to grooming for puppies under 6 months old. This service focuses on getting your puppy comfortable with the grooming process while maintaining their hygiene.

Puppy-Specific Care Includes:

  • Extra gentle handling and patience
  • Shortened session with breaks as needed
  • Mild puppy shampoo
  • Basic face and sanitary trimming
  • Nail trimming with positive reinforcement
  • Ear cleaning and inspection
  • Introduction to grooming tools
  • Plenty of treats and praise
  • Light brushing and deshedding
  • Special puppy bandana

Duration: 1 - 1.5 hours with breaks as needed

Age Requirement: Puppies must be at least 12 weeks old and have completed second vaccinations
